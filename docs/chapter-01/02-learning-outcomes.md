# Chapter 1: Learning Outcomes

## Remember Level
- Define the term "Physical AI" and distinguish it from traditional AI approaches
- Identify the key historical phases in the development of Physical AI
- List the core principles underlying embodied intelligence
- Enumerate the main application domains of Physical AI

## Understand Level
- Explain the relationship between embodied intelligence and physical interaction
- Describe the historical evolution from symbolic AI to embodied cognition
- Summarize the key differences between Physical AI and traditional AI approaches
- Compare the characteristics of traditional AI vs. Physical AI systems

## Apply Level
- Identify scenarios where Physical AI provides advantages over traditional AI
- Implement basic physics simulation with embodied agents
- Apply the concept of morphological computation to a simple robotic problem
- Use sensing and actuation loops in a physical system

## Analyze Level
- Compare Physical AI with symbolic AI and connectionist AI approaches
- Analyze the advantages of embodiment in intelligent systems
- Evaluate the trade-offs between accuracy and efficiency in Physical AI systems
- Examine the role of environmental coupling in intelligent behavior

## Evaluate Level
- Assess the potential impact of Physical AI on robotics and AI fields
- Critique traditional AI approaches in the context of physical tasks
- Evaluate the effectiveness of embodied systems for specific applications
- Consider the societal implications of Physical AI advancement

## Create Level
- Design a basic concept for a Physical AI application
- Create a simulation that demonstrates Physical AI principles
- Develop a framework for integrating sensing, physics, and control
- Plan a Physical AI solution for a practical robotic problem
