# Chapter 1: Learning Outcomes

After completing this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Define Physical AI and distinguish it from traditional AI approaches
2. Explain the key characteristics that differentiate Physical AI systems
3. Identify various applications of Physical AI in real-world scenarios
4. Describe the main challenges faced in developing Physical AI systems
5. Understand the relationship between Physical AI and humanoid robotics
6. Recognize the interdisciplinary nature of Physical AI development
7. Analyze the potential impact of Physical AI on society and industry

These learning outcomes will be reinforced throughout the book with practical examples and exercises.
