# PHYSICAL AI & HUMANOID ROBOTICS TEXTBOOK - RESEARCH REFERENCES

## 1. CORE TEXTBOOK REFERENCES

### 1.1 Physical AI & Embodied Intelligence
- **"Physical Intelligence" by Sangbae Kim et al.** - Core concepts about the intersection of physical systems and AI
- **"Introduction to Autonomous Robots" by Giorgio Piccini et al.** - Foundation for understanding autonomous systems
- **"Robotics: Control, Sensing, Vision, and Intelligence" by Fu, Gonzalez, and Lee** - Classic robotics text with mathematical foundations

### 1.2 Humanoid Robotics
- **"Humanoid Robotics: A Reference" by Ambarish Goswami and Prahlad Vadakkepat** - Comprehensive coverage of humanoid-specific challenges
- **"Humanoid Robots: Modeling and Control" by Dragomir N. Nenchev, Atsushi Konno, Hirohisa Chinami** - Technical details on humanoid control systems
- **"Humanoid Motion Planning for Dynamic Quadrupedal Robots" by Shuuji Kajita et al.** - Motion planning methods for legged robots

### 1.3 ROS 2 and Robot Middleware
- **"Programming Robots with ROS" by Morgan Quigley, Brian Gerkey, and William Smart** - Comprehensive ROS programming guide
- **"Effective Robotics Programming with ROS" by Anil Mahtani, Luis Sánchez Crespo, and Enrique Fernández Perdomo** - Best practices in ROS development
- **Official ROS 2 Documentation (Humble Hawksbill)** - Latest standards and practices
